HOW TO IMPORT PRESETS

1. Make sure you have the desktop version of Adobe Lightroom installed. If not, you can download it HERE.

2. Extract the .zip folder by double-clicking it.

3. Open Lightroom, Click on the "Lightroom" (for Mac) or "Edit" (for Windows) tab at the top left, then click "Preferences"

4. Click on the "Presets" tab, then click "Show Lightroom Presets Folder", Navigate to: Lightroom > Develop Presets.

5. Copy the folders that were extracted into the "Develop Presets" folder

6. Extract the .zip folder by double-clicking it.

7. Close Lightroom and reopen Lightroom

8. Click the "Develop" module in the top right. Your presets will be displayed on the left side. Happy Editing!
